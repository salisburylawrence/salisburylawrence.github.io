Case study

eLight v2.0 (LED refurbishment lifecycle platform)

eLight v2.0 was developed from the ground up to cover every key stage of an LED lighting refurbishment project. Through careful system architecture, the platform enables automation across Survey, Solution Design, Mobilisation, Delivery, Completion and Aftercare — with live shared data providing a single source of truth.

Phase 1 (Survey, Design & Propose) launched in June 2025. One Reality is now supporting Phase 2 (Mobilise, Deliver, Complete & Maintain) to carry design intent through to implementation and ongoing improvement.
