Proof of Work

Retrofit Delivery Operations

A national energy saving retrofit business operating a high-volume survey-to-delivery programme. Disconnected systems, manual handoffs and duplicated effort were creating a cost-per-project ceiling that was suppressing margin despite strong revenue. A full operational redesign unlocked capacity without increasing headcount.

Phase 1 — Survey to Proposal

Workflow mapping identified manual data re-entry, inconsistent site reporting and approval bottlenecks as the primary cost drivers. Redesigned with structured survey workflows and automated proposal generation — cutting cost per survey-to-proposal from £3,390 to £1,690.

Phase 2 — Closed Won to Delivered

Full digitisation of the mobilisation and delivery workflow. Cost per delivered project reduced from £11,250 to £5,630. With sales capacity to match, the same operational team doubled project throughput — lifting annual margin from £847K to £3.8M.
